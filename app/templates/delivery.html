Delivery and Warranty

Delivery

We offer reliable and efficient delivery services for your convenience. Our delivery options include:

  • Standard delivery: Arrives within 5-7 business days.
  • Express delivery: Arrives within 2-3 business days (additional fees may apply).
  • International delivery: Shipping options available for international orders (additional fees may apply).

Please note that delivery times may vary depending on your location and product availability. We will provide you with a tracking number to monitor the status of your delivery.

Warranty

We stand behind the quality of our products. All items come with a standard warranty of 1 year from the date of purchase. The warranty covers manufacturing defects and workmanship issues.

If you encounter any problems with your purchase during the warranty period, please contact our customer support team. We will assess the issue and provide a suitable solution, which may include repair, replacement, or refund.

The warranty does not cover damages resulting from misuse, improper handling, or normal wear and tear. It is essential to follow the product care instructions provided to ensure the longevity of your purchase.
